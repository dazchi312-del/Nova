The corpus contains records from two generations of the Nova engine. Generation 1 ("lean") wrote iteration records with exec_result and scalar scores, alongside experiment manifests with score_progression and thresholds. Generation 2 ("rich") wrote iteration records with separate sandbox/dreamer/reflector timings and multi-axis scores, alongside experiment manifests with goal, initial_hypothesis, and structured per-iteration score arrays. Both generations are unversioned (v0). Migration to v1 preserves both faithfully and tags each with its detected generation.
